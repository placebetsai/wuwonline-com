Israel Joffe

Israel Joffe's editorial home — media-executive credits at Fox 5, Newsweek, Fox 29, NewsBreak, FDIC firefighter writing, and the IT specialist work that backs WUW's broadcast and archive infrastructure.

Israel Joffe is the long-time technologist and media operator behind a working network of New York publishing properties. He has trained inside Gleason's Gym for years alongside the WUW family — many of the dated /israel-joffe/ entries on this site are field reports from those sessions.

His personal site israeljoffe.com collects the press credits, broadcast appearances, and the DocumentCloud filings he's released over the years — including the Atlantic City research and the letter from the Lubavitcher Rebbe. It's the natural starting point for anyone trying to follow his work outside the ring.

He also keeps a parallel BJJ and fitness journal at israeljoffe.org — the gym-and-mat side of the same training discipline that brings him to WUW.
