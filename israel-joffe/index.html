About

Israel Joffe is a New York–based media executive, IT specialist, and FDIC-credentialed firefighter whose work has appeared in Fox 5, Newsweek, Fox 29, and NewsBreak. He's been training at Gleason's Gym alongside the WUW family for years — the dated entries below are field reports from those sessions and the related coverage that came out of them.

For the full picture of his work outside the ring, his editorial home is israeljoffe.com (media, press, IT) and the BJJ-and-fitness side is at israeljoffe.org.
