The Legend of Johnny Rodz

Born José Rodriguez, Johnny “The Unpredictable” Rodz is one of professional wrestling’s most revered figures. A cornerstone of the old-school MSG circuit, Rodz spent decades competing at the highest levels before transitioning into his true calling: building the next generation of wrestling superstars.

His ability to make opponents look like a million dollars was an art form — a quality that defines his teaching philosophy at World of Unpredictable Wrestling.

WWE Hall of Fame: Class of 1996

In 1996, Johnny Rodz was inducted into the WWE Hall of Fame, presented by his longtime friend Arnold Skaaland — recognition of his lifetime of contributions to professional wrestling.
